Overall Rating

Categories: Role-playing /Strategy game

Brief Introduction to the story:

The main character Corrin is captured by Nohr, and get visited by 4 siblings of Nohrian Royal Family. They treat Corrin very well and seem establishing good relationship with each others. However, the blood family discovers Corrin and call Corrin to return Hoshido, while these two contries are combating together. The right to choose is on the player's hand.

　
Rating -
Design/artwork: ★★★★★　5/5
Music: ★★★★★　5/5
System: ★★★★☆　4/5
Story: ★★☆☆☆　2/5

For design:
Yusuke Kozaki is the character designer. The design is done with high quality, including the coloring, styling, and even the picture structuring.

For music:
This kind of game may require long time playing in a battle so the quality of battling music can be quite important. The music quality is quite high in this game and it would be quite hard to get bored on it in a short period. I appreciate some of the Hoshido theme in Japanese style.

For system:
The system control is easy and convenient. The system would remind some hot keys in the screen so the control would not be too difficult.
The battling system can be challeging for the beginner with the first Fire Emblem/ strategy game. It gets complicated compared to Fire Emblem Awaken. There are extra types of weapons and need memorization.

For story:
It is not recommended to put too much expectation on the main story, but the bond talk can be good for some pairs of characters.

===========================================================

People who is recommmended to play this game:

If you like to see the relationship (bond) with some characters on the game, you can go for all parts/ part(s) with these characters of the game.

If you would love to enjoy the strategy game, you can especially go for Conquest (Dark Night Kingdom).

People who is NOT recommmended to play this game:

If you focus so much on the main story, you may skip this game.

The lower parts of the article will introduce the game in different titles.



Birthright (White Night Kingdom)

For the story: This part has the relatively better written story compared to two other parts of the game, but it is not good to put too much expectation.....

For the difficulty: This part has lower level of difficuly compared to Conquest even with the same difficulty settings. The equipped weapons of the enemies are not that strong or with many different types. Even the enemy AI act less clever... If the battle seems difficult, players can choose to level up with provided free map(s).

===========================================================

People who is recommmended to play this part:

You are not familiar with the system / beginner

You love some of the characters in this part, which he/she have bond talk or have chance to talk in the main story.

People who is NOT recommmended to play this part:

You love the Norh family more than Hosido family

You love so much on the Norh limited character(s) over available characters in Hoshido

===========================================================



Conquest (Dark Night Kingdom)

For the story: I think the story of this part is the worst among three titles...

For the difficulty: This is the most difficult one among three titles. The number of battle and amount of resources are limited in usual play style. Players have to plan well on resources purchase and uses. The AI act very clever compared to Birthright, and the enemies are going to take your unit(s) down seriously. It is your cabability to plan every move with the consideration of enemies' existing weapons, scope of move, scope of attack, and the triggering point for enemies to take action (move).... It can be life saving if you have the interrupted save in a correct point.

===========================================================

People who is recommmended to play this part:

You are confident / persistent to difficult battles

You love some of the characters in this part, which he/she have bond talk or have chance to talk in the main story.

People who is NOT recommmended to play this part:

You love the Hoshido family more than Norh family

You love so much on the Hoshido limited character(s) over available characters in Norh
