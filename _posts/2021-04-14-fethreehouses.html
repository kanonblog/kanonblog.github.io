Overall Rating

Categories: Tactical role-playing game

Brief Introduction of the story:

The main character Byleth (Male)/ Byleth (Female) offers help to students of Officers Academy, including Edelgard (Leader of Black Eagles House), Dimitri (Leader of Blue Lion House), and Claude (Leader of Golden Deer House), during the battle to bandits. The main character gets invited to be a teacher of Officers Academy, and be able to teach one of the houses. However, the leaders of three houses have influences on the continent of Fódlan, your choice may lead the continent to a different future....

　
Rating -
Design/artwork: ★★★★★　5/5
Music: ★★★★☆　4/5
System: ★★★★☆　4/5
Story: ★★★★☆　4/5

For design,
The character design is good in school uniform and other clothes depend.
The 3D model looks really good and it has vivid 3D movie in the game.

For music, I think they are above average/ high quality but I prefer the ones in FE Fate.

For system,
There are more tactical options during the battles, which enriches the gameplay experience.
There has a good enhancement for visual indicator during battles, which is having arrow pointing to the target of the enemies. This makes the movement of enemies being more predictable.
The best part should be the rolling back system, which can reduce the time of resetting the game if players lose unit(s) during battle.

For story,
I think there is a huge improvement compared to FE Fate.
The bond talk seems more meaningful and even have the additional information to the background settings of the game. You may know the living of each areas better.
It would be so satisfatory if you are willing to infer what is happening through the story with the gathered clues. To enjoy the story fully, there is a recommended order to enter each routes. I only know the story of the main routes with the presence of the leaders, so I will state my own recommendation in accordance to my gameplay experience.

There is the recommended order to enter each route, if you wanna enjoy the story of all houses.

(First)Black Eagles - Crimson Flower => (Second) Blue Lions - Azure Moon => (Third)Golden Deer - Verdant Wind

People who is recommmended to play this game:

If you are a beginner of tactical game, this game should be a good entry point with lower difficulty.

If you like some characters in this game, you may enjoy the bond talks and the conversation in the stories.

People who is NOT recommmended to play this game:

If you are totally unacceptable to the bisexual related matter, you should avoid this game. (There is a way to avoid: Only level up one pairs of characters with highest level of bond.)

All the house leaders have their own goal to achieve, which you need to go through the route to know.

The lower parts will briefly introduce the characters and atmosphere of each house.



Black Eagles - Crimson Flower

First impression to the house: Elite

　
Edelgard: Always observe the cabability and performance of the professor. She is quite cute when she trust the main character but some people may think she is cruel. Heir apparent of the Adrestian Empire.
Hubert: is loyal to Edelgard. Treat the main character with a degree of hostile in the beginning. He is only playable in this route (Not even in Silver Snow).
Ferdinand: Always call his own name. Personally think he is a funny guy. His mind seems quite straight-forward without considering deep.
Linhardt: Always feel sleeply and seem clever.
Caspar: is a nice guy and considerable. Do not seem good at thinking.
Bernadetta: Always wanna stay in her own room. SUPER CUTE.
Dorothea: should be the only commoner from Adrestian Empire. She is thoughtful and nice.
Petra: is the only person from Brigid, and she is a Princess. She does not speak full Fódlan language usually, but she is so kind and thoughtful.



Blue Lions - Azure Moon

First impression to the house: Serious

　
Dimitri: acts polite to the main character but he is actually super strong in terms of stregth. Crown prince of the Holy Kingdom of Faerghus.
Debue: is loyal to Dimitri and always look calm. He is only playable in this route.
Felix: Always see him in arena. Pursue strength over all other things. Childhood friends with Dimitri, Sylvain and Ingrid.
Ashe: Act like an average personality but look so reliable and nice.
Sylvain: Always pursue girls/ladies... If the main character is Byleth(female), and is teaching other house, the main character can ask him to join immediately without effort......He is a strong unit though. Childhood friends with Dimitri, Felix and Ingrid.
Mercedes: Act so girlie and calm. Have friendship with Annette.
Annette: is very energetic. Have friendship with Mercedes.
Ingrid: is thoughtful to others and wanna be a typical knight. Childhood friends with Dimitri, Sylvain and Felix.



Golden Deer - Verdant Wind

First impression to the house: Easy going and Relaxed

　
Claude: (upside down) Look nice and easy going but actually he is a tactician. Heir to House Riegan.
Lorenz: Always talk about how to live like a nobel. Interesting to see the bond talk with Ferdinand.
Raphael: Always see him in the dining hall. He is a warm person.
Ignatz: Like painting.
Lysithea: Always wanna act like an adult. Cute and capable.
Marianne: Look quiet and have low self confidence.
Hilda: Look girlie and cute! Although she always act lazy, she is a capable person when she is willing to do something.
Leonie: Act boyish.
