Overall rating

Category: Tactical RPG

Brief introduction of the story: Protagonist gets the invitation from his friend Daichi Shijima to Nicaea β ver, which enable the users to see the video of their friends' death. When two of them leave the examination venue and get into the subway, they meet another schoolmate, Io Nitta. They receive e-mails containing a video that shows a coming train crashing into the subway station, while three of them get crushed. Protagonist confirms his willing to live and gets attacked by the summoned demons. Three of them fight against a group of demon and get installed an application called Demon Summoning Program in the mobile phone. However, this tool helps the main characters staying alive and get related to the things going to happen in the next few days....

　
Rating -
Design/artwork: ★★★★★　5/5
Music: ★★★★☆　4/5
System: ★★★☆☆　3/5
Story: ★★★★☆　4/5

For the design/artwork, I think the character design is excellent, as the character designer is a famous illustrator Suzuhito Yasuda.

For the music, I think the music is not that memoriable, so I cannot judge that very well. I think they are on average level.

For the system, the system should be almost the same with the original version in 2009. It may not be fair to compared with the former games, so I skip it here. However, I think it is incovenient to have NO skipping option to the seen conversations.....

There is an enhancement compared to the original version. The difficulty can be selected to an easier mode, which reduces the time spent on the tactical part and fasten the gameplay. It is very GOOD when players want to play the game repeatively for the route selection.

For the story, if you are not expecting a very deep and serious story, and treat this game as a bonding game, you will get satisfied. I kinda treat this game as like Gal game along with tactical system. The bonding events of the male characters are good written as well, so please do not really treat it as a Gal game.



Suggestions/Tips

This section is for players totally new to the game.

This game would not instruct the way to optimize your action for better battling result, so you need to beware of the best move by yourself. I am not sure whether the game wiki would provide any good hints.

I would like to remind that when the playable character finishes the movement and attack, there is still available to take action by the demons equipped by the characters. I didn't aware that and made the gameplay hard.....

Then, if you wanna have a smoother gameplay, it would be better to follow the crack guide, and make sure to have the every single skill cracked in every battle, especially for the rare skills.

It would be nice to have your active members having higher bonding level, so you can have the benefits like sharing the cracking slot (which I think is the most important part).

There is the must-have skills in my opinion:

[For high magic character]

Drain: Absorb HP and MP from one enemy.

Holy Dance: Inflicts Magic-relative Almighty damage on random targets.

Megidolaon: Inflicts heavy Almighty damage on all enemies.

(auto skill)

Magic Yang: MP costs for both teams halved.

Health Save: HP costs for both teams reduced by 75%.

Racial skill for demon - Blood Treaty: Restores MP to team (dependent on damage dealt)

[For high strength character]

Hassohappa: Inflicts HP-dependent Physical damage on all enemies.

Deathbound: Inflicts HP/Level/Vitality-dependent Physical damage on random targets.

Multi-Hit: Inflicts light Physical damage to one enemy 2-7 times based on Agility. Hit more when the equipped character has higher agility.

Multi-Strike: same with Multi-Hit but this skill hits all enemies.

(Passive Skill)

Pierce: Physical attacks cannot be resisted, nullified, or absorbed.

(auto skill)

Health Save: HP costs for both teams reduced by 75%.


Also, it is very interesting to see the difference of effective attacking pattern in different time point of the game. This is only my point of view:

For the beginning:

Normal attack would be effective enough

For the middle:

Magic attack like magic-dance series or Almighty attack would be effective

Approaching to the end:

The physical skills would be effective with passive skill Pierce.

It would be important to have magic and physical attacker in the same team (4 members) so everything can go smooth in normal situation.
