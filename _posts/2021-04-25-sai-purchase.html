Procedure to purchase & activiate

Please be notified that the purchase procedure may not be accurate enough compared to the latest implementation. Please follow the intruction in the official website and this note is only for reference.

1. Confirm the license information in the website

2. Click $ Purchase Software License for PaintTool SAI Ver.1 $

3. Read the details and click "agree" button

4. Fill the Quantity & E-mail addree & Name & your address respectively

5. Choose the payment method.

6. Confirm the verficaition code with the filled e-mail address.

7. (Paypal) Click the Paypal button and get directed to the paypal login interface

8. Finish the payment

9. Obtain the "Software License Number" and "Certificate Download Password" in the subsequent e-mail

10. Go to Download Software License Certificate page

11. Input the "Software License Number" and "Certificate Download Password" from the recevied e-mail

12. Check "Your computer's System ID" by opening SAI tool->(navigation bar)others->System ID

13. Copy the system ID and place into the "Your computer's System ID" field

14. Click "Download Software Licence Certificate" button

15. Save the file into the directory with SAI.exe [SAI's program folder] (which is usually in "C:\PaintToolSAI")

16. The trail-days output should disppear.

Note: (I didn't try this option) There is an option to reissue the licence by navigating to "Reissue Software License Information". However, please be aware of the limitation of available counts for downloading the certificate during a time period, as indicated in the website.
