**Rean** - This Rean is properly the leader of Imperial Liberation Front. In the original version, the school takes place in Heimdallr. There is a residential area of nobels, so Rean properly want to approach nobels and enter the school.

**Alisa** - When you observe the clothes of this Alisa, you can observe the similarity of cloth designs with Olivert and Alfin. Please compare those with their cold steel 3 outfits in red. She has a blonde hair as well, so I assume her being a loyal family. Also, Alisa is a sword wielder, along with a sword with weird shape. This should have some meaning here, but I don't have enough clues. I assume the use of the sword is for activating the Divine Knights as a key.

**Jusis** - His family is one of the Four Great Houses. He is properly an opponent, or a temperary opponent in the prototype version. The reasons are not strong enough, but the following is my obvervation. 

When battles end, he always mention lines like "commitment to nobel obligation". Also, He has a stronger order than Rean called "Noble Command". Those look like the clues of being an enemy, and join after some events. He may be one of the opponents against Rean's plot. He may get involved to the conflicts between nobels and commoners.

**Vita** - she works with Crow in the current version, but she may be the witch of the Hexen Clan to Rean in prototype version. She may get into a sub-heroine position in this version!

<p><div class="left-margin" id="set-C"><h2>Possible Story Flow</h2></div></p>
The followings are my imagination of possible story flow for prototype version:

1. Olivert starts a new class called class VII. He know the entry of Rean, and he want to take this as an advantage. Olivert may try to seek union with Rean, or stop the acts of him.
2. Assign Alisa and Jusis to class VII. Olivert may hope to gain information from Alisa about the moves of Rean and Jusis. Also, It may be a better result for Alisa to build good relationship with both of them, so more things can go as planned.
3. Rean fulfills some of his objectives and leave the school. He acts as C.
4. Rean meets Vita, and be able to awake the Divine Knight(s).
5. Enter Nord Highlands for the Divine Knight(s), so Rean can become the pilot.
6. Follow the flow of what Crow does in cold steel 1-2
7. The members of class VII try to convince Rean. Alisa tries to bring Rean back.
8. It is time to confront Osborne, like in cold steel 2 and 4. All Divine Knights come out and explain the story.
9. End

I guess the contents of coldsteel 3-4 are almost new from the original plot, but the final results may be the same to the prototype.
