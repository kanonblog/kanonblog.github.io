Overall rating

As you may know, this series is developed during 2004-2007 in the original version. Therefore, it may not be fair to judge the design/artwork and system. I will only place the comments regarding music here, and the story would be rated in separate sections.

For the music, it would be rated ★★★★★(5/5) if there needs to be a score given.

I think the music has good rhythm and memoriable. This kind of music sounds special and I do not have enough knowledge to tell the category... At least the music worthes to be listened, and it would be quite enjoyable to have those music during battle/map.


Below is the table to see whether you are recommended to this game.

===========================================================

People who is recommmended to play this game:

If you like to see main protagonists going out of a fixed district, and have adventure around the story setting.

If you like to see the characters having personal growth in a relatively long timeline, you may enjoy playing this whole series.

If you are interested about story development from later series, you may find your information here.

If you like to enjoy a typical JRPG, you are welcome.

People who is NOT recommmended to play this game:

If you prefer the design/artwork/system/expression of former games instead, you may need to adopt the difference or step back.

If you wanna know the final plot of the whole trails series, it would be better for you to play the later ones. Here is only the starting point of the whole trails series.

Trails in the sky

Brief introduction of the story: The main protagonist Estelle goes on a journey with her adopted brother Joshua to become a qualified Bracer. They are going to face the plot behind the Liberl Kingdom....

Positive feedback to this game:

Based on the Japanese version, I think this one is the best written one. The conversations are very smooth and interesting.

The characters are well developed along with the timeline. They act like a single person instead of a character with a typical character setting.

The main protagonists Estelle and Joshua learn and experience during the story and they will have some changes until the end of the story. It can be quite satisfactory to see their growth.

Neutral Feedback:

The game has smaller scale of incidents compared to former trails series.

The generation gap exists in the perspective of story expression. I think it is good but there is a bit different of how to describe a thing.

This game tells the story in a slower pace, which I find enjoyable to know each characters better.

It would be so inspiring to see their jorney until the end, but you know, it still has the next episode.


Anyway, the character interaction is so good in this episode, and it is a starting point for me to pursue this series.



Trails in the sky SC

Brief introduction(Spoiler): Estelle wants to find out Joshua after the event occured in the last episode. The story will reveal the past of Joshua and the plot behind...

Positive Feedback:

The last game leave some problems unsolved, and this part reveals a larger picture of the plot.

This episode gives good explaination to the incidents in the story, and there are new characters from another authority, which enables players to know the scale of the story.

The characteristics of some characters are enriched with the description of their past and rivals.

Neutral Feedback:

I find the writing style different compared to the last episode. This episode is more approaching to the former trails series. If you are a big fan to former trails series and feel good with the last episode, you may find this episode good.

It may look quite repeatitive to walk through the Liberl Kingdom again......



Trails in the sky 3rd

Brief introduction(Spoiler): Kevin supported the trip of Estelle in last episode. He becomes the main protagonist in this episode, and you will find his real job and the hidden duties of Septian Church.

The main protagonists are no longer Estelle and Joshua, but they will still appear in this episode.

This episode is more a fan-disc and provide side-story of existing characters. Players need to unlock the doors for pieces of story under some conditions. Also, it is kinda like a Dungeon game. Players need to go through the dungeon of Phantasma instead of walking through a territory.

Positive Feedback:

You can know more about the unmentioned story of each characters, and even for some NPCs.

There are some hints related to later series.

There are new playable characters based on previous episodes, like Renne, and Richard !!! They are very capable in my opinion.

It has higher flexibility to choose the members.

Negative Feedback:

I don't think here is a lot of new story. This game may not make you feeling worthy to play if you are not that enthusiastic to the current characters/plot. The former series reveal more pieces of plot in my point of view...

It would be better to check the content of the doors before opening it... I read some spoiler and avoided.......... I am not sure whether the English version has made some changes.

Neutral:

It will be still good to play if you are a big fan of trails series and wanna experience every single journey and discover the hints for the latest plot.

It is better to avoid some doors if the content is ...............



Last words

If you like the traditional JRPG or trail series, just go for it!! (except 3rd)
