Master and Mashu get sent to an idol Singularity. Being an idol represents the strength here. Ms Crane tried to save the master and mashu from a battle, but the attempt fails. E-chan is acting as an idol to save the master and Mashu. Then, they go to the living of E-chan together, to prepare becoming a better idol.

The team AxXxS manages to get the victory in the grail live! However, something strange happens, when another Ms Crane appears. Master and Mashu return to the beginning of the event. They decide to find out Ms Crane for more clues. Ms Crane finds her restriction, and try to break it. Then her memory returns. However, Medb and Tamamo cat have no memory of the journey of AxXxS anymore.

E-chan tries to fight against the another Ms Crane, and get the holy grail. Players can know the background of "this" Ms Crane, wanting to give return to her previous master. Her master passed away, and no one knew the talent of her previous master, of being good in design. She decided to hold events along with idols, and let them dress good in stage. 

Ms Crane along with master has the objective to seek for an idol with good potential to win in the grail live, and everything gets reset after the winner comes out. When master knows her intention, she/he suggests to hold an excellent stage, with all idols in this event, to farewell Ms Crane.

<p><div class="left-margin" id="set-D"><h2>Last Words</h2></div></p>
I really love the story in the event. Every character is being vivid and try hard to meet their goals. I like the enthusiasm, hope, and persistence, for them confronting new challeges. The story is written in good pace, and have good turning points. Ms Crane has a lovable personality, although she is being a villain in the story.

And the costumes are cute !
