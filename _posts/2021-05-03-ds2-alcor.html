Experience of the second round

This was my second round of the game, so I had stronger demons available. Also, the restriction of getting less battling experience was cancelled.The level of the members increased very fast compared to the first round, even though no free map battle was conducted.

My strategy to become stronger was simply waiting until having enough Macca, and exchanged the desirable demons from Demon Compendium. If the amount of demons are sufficient for battling, I will try to search the demons and fuse a stronger one based on the demons called from Demon Compendium.

Almost the time, I sent only the protagonist to the battle and earn more Macca with the equipped passive skill (Moneybags).

It indeed save time in the battle along with strong demons and cracked skills. However, there is no skipping function on the scenario and battles. Players skill need to invest time on bonding events for desirable endings, and collect the playable characters after the route selection.



Overall reflection

Default members in this route: Saiduq, (join afterward) Daichi, Io

Daichi and Io should join the team regardless of the bond level, according to the wiki.

Posistive Feedback:

It is so new to see protagonist sleeping in the station without accompany of any of the existing members after the route selection.

During the battles, the existing members put the doubts of who is Saiduq at first.... This pattern is so suprised for me. I realize that not everyone gets a chance to see him during the story.

Finally get a chance to have Saiduq as a battling member! He is a strong member as he has resistants against Fire and Electricity while also immune to Curse.

Saiduq acts funny while he is serious during the conversations. He always disappear after conversations, and sometimes move forward without using his feet.

I get to know his background better... He is indeed a key character, who has a lot of core information to the main story. His route is like complmenting the details of the main story.

I can feel full of hope in the ending, although it would not be easy for modern citizens to live in ancient era... (kinda related to Triangulum Arc)

Negative Feedback:

Why don't Saiduq tell me that he will no longer accompany the protagonist in the ending!!!! As Daichi say, it is so lonely to have a friend to disappear.....



Main characters in this route

Note: The lines are from Triangulum Arc.

Al Saiduq - I trust the posiblities of you guys and humanity.

This should be the only route for Saiduq to join the team in Septentrione Arc. He will definitely die in other routes accorindg to the wiki, even in Daichi's route. This route is available only if the protagonist provides hints to him for a new possibility.

He is kinda a humanity saver, who provides fire and knowledge to human. This part can tell his caring to humanity. That is the reason of making Nicaea, so that people can have the weapon fighting against Septentriones. This act saves the life of the main protagonist, and offer him an opportunity to choose the order of the afterward world.

Becuase Saiduq does not even have imagination of joining route selection, he does not show good preparation to the exisiting problems. It is so different to Yamato, but it is so good to see characters helping each other to solve the problems.

He is like the position of EVA's Kaworu. If you live along with your usual life, he will need to obey the Polaris and die. This game provides a route for him, but he will disappear any way... It just make me kinda sad.... One of my objective of entering this route is to make him alive...

In this route, making a new world means all possibilities, so I cannot find much to mention. Saiduq's action is not arguable compared to Yamato's route.


Daichi - I cannot get used to this kind of experience even though I try for several times.

He follows the selection of protagonist, and he makes good interaction to Saiduq during the route. I love his lines so much when Saiduq disappears in the ending... Throughout the whole route, I think he really treat Saiduq as a good friend...


Io - I would not get afraid with all of you...!

She does not have much lines during this story, but I am so glad for her joining.


I get all available characters joining the team in this round, and the joined members would have their own lines to their initial joined team. It is so GOOOOD to see their unity towards the same goal. However, I am not going to describe them here. See if I still have motivation to finish the remaining parts.



Comparsion of main characters in Yamato's Route

Yamato

He cannot join in this route, and he persists his own ideal until the end... This is quite sad for me...

He invites the main protagonist to join so hard, and even reveal the truth of Saiduq being a Septentrione.

He shows his obsession to main protagonist. Although I think his choice is correct, the route selected by protagonist will eventually come true in usual....

I think Yamato is the type of character with more description while the protagonist does not choose his route... There is the content involving the imagination of the interaction between Yamato and Saiduq before the incident. I think this part is included in the content of the animation.


Makoto

She reveals her true will against the meritocracy, while the main protagonist invites her to join. I am glad that she gets an opportunity to pursue her ideal.


Fumi

She is so eager to join while there is a new possibility, and unknown factors in the new world. It shows her strong desire towards doing research.

Also, she is really helpful in summoning trumpeter even though she is not supposed to join the route. She is indeed a nice person.


Keita

I think he does not care so much if he can train himself to become stronger.
