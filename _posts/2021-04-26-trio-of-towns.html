Overall rating

Brief Introduction to the story: The main Protagonist loved to get in touch with nature and animals so much. She/he obtains the the permission from her/his families to his/her auntie Frank's living. The main Protagonist is able to continue his/her farming life when the given trials are overcame....

　
Rating -
Design/artwork: ★★★★★　5/5
Music: ★★★★★　5/5
System: ★★★☆☆　3/5
Story: ★★★★★　5/5

For the design/artwork, the character designs look normal and ordinary in the first glance, but they look really good along with the time passage. Also, the bachelorettes/bachelors should have single picture well portrayed. You may take a look if you are interested.

For the music, the music is very memoriable and have good match to the atmosphere. The music style is more of relaxing style. It would be enjoyable to listen when you are walking through the maps or going on the events.

For the system, I think the system is not that convenient and require repeatitive works, BUT it is the point to have funny farming life, right? Usually I do not prefer this gaming style, but the scenario is too good this time....

For the story, this is REALLY good written. While the bonding level increases, you can observe changes to the conversations. The conversations include more personal feelings, caring and the inner views. You can feel the kindness of them and it shows the closer relationship in real. The scenario does not omit every single village, so you can know each person better and feel like they are a real person. They are just like living in the village together, having the regular life, having the different cultural background, and accept the difference of each other. I kinda felt healed when I played this game.

I would like to note that regardless of the gender, the characters are very nice, kind, and friendly from the beginning of the game. This game can surely bring relief to your life if you like this kind of character settings! (in my opinion; The farming may not be easy to everyone though.)

People who is recommmended to play this game:

If you are new to this series, this game provides good instruction so that the player can adopt the gameplay smoothly.

If you like some characters in this game, you can enjoy the dialogues in a relatively long run.

People who is NOT recommmended to play this game:

If you totally cannot endure the repeatitive farming work, you may feel bored to play this game.

Suggestions/Tips

This section is for the newbie with no experience in this game.

The first village available is Westown, followed by Lulukoko and Tsuyukusa. The conditions for opening is the time passage.

It would be good to visit the mining point every day when you get a hammer. The obtained materials should be kept for later uses, such as upgrading farming tools.

Every kind of crops should be kept for a certain amount in order to have enough ingredients for festivals/town link rank.

My own experience to stop playing the game:
I always get too hurry to fulfill my wills, such as to make clothes and having a lot of animals. This kind of activities make me exhausted to tackle with the tasks everyday in the game. It may be good to slow down the gaming pace, and you may enjoy the game in a longer period.



Last Words

I think this game is very worthy to play. If you are okay with the repeatitive works, you may have a try!
