<p><div class="left-margin" id="set-A"><h2>Stereotypes of IT</h2></div></p>

1. IT industry is not limited to programming

    It is well-known for people about IT in programming fields. There are some other fields like IT-auditing, web programmer, networking, database, cloud computing, system administration, information security, etc.

2. IT person needs to be excellent in mathematics

    It is reasonable for IT related workers having a degree of mathematical capabilities, but it is not necessarily required to be extremely good at mathematics. It depends on your working or learning subjects in IT.

<p><div class="left-margin" id="set-C"><h2>Career</h2></div></p>

It is my own observation of IT job. You may have different situations, but please take a look if you choose IT majoy solely for career.

1. Difficulty of IT job: IT advances so fast

    - May require continuous learning to new technologies<br>
        IT technology advances so fast, so the knowledge in the university may not be good enough to face the challenges ahead. It looks normal to learn during, and after works.

2. Job nature: Need communication and hard work, while may need extra hours for support.

    - People may imagine being a programmer and shut the mouth off in the work. However, you may need to negotiate things with colleagues, or talk during conferences and meetings. It is quite challegeing to load the commputing logics in mind and speak human languages in mouth.

    - Your stand-by hours may be required to ensure system operations, which means you may have less convenience to schedule your after-work time. 

If you are willing to learn fast changing technologies, having good communication skills, being cooperative to others, you may consider to take IT as your career! I think the IT jobs look quite challeaging, but it depends on individual situations, and your passion to the industry.
