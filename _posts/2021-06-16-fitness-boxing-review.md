<p><div class="left-margin" id="set-A"><h2>Highlights of the system</h2></div></p>

1. Outfit rewards

    Along with the exericse hours accumulation, players can obtain the costumes and accessaries for the trainers. It can be treated like an award of your hard work.

2. Reminder of your persistence

    The game records the consecutive days of exercise, and allow players to put a stamp after daily training. This mechanism reminds your hard work of doing regular exercise.

3. Auto-calculation of Calorie and days of workouts

    Your hard work is represented by numbers. You can estimate how the game helps yourself burning Calorie along with the days.

4. Attractive Trainers

    For Japanese version, it hires <u>famous voice actors (JP)</u>, to guide players during the training. You can listen the guiding words with those voices. It is like to have a person doing exercise with you. 

    You are able to <u>change the trainer</u>. You may choose a female or male trainer. They have a bit difference on their characteristics. Their words are quite encouraging.

    There are <u>an amount of clothes available for trainers</u>. You may change their clothing, while the options are opened.

5. Simple operation

    Once players select the trainning courses, the system will generate the courses with the chosen criteria every time. There needs no user intervention, and reduces the hestitation towards working out every day. If you want to change the training options, the time duration and trained body parts can be changed every time. If you are motivated to work out more than daily courses, or you want to focus more on dedicated body parts, the extra training courses are available.

<p><div class="left-margin" id="set-B"><h2>Personal Experience</h2></div></p>

<h3>Good exeprience</h3>

1. Do not require much space and time for a training

2. Change training plan easily

    - It is able to change the training plan with personal needs. 
    - Workload is predictable and controllable
    - It would not be too stressful to play for a long period.

3. Reasonable price

    - Do not need to spend extra expenses for equipments.
    - Longer play time means more worthiness.

4. The training courses enhance the difficulties gradually

    - Provide time for players to adapt the movement.

<h3>Cons</h3>

1. The exercise courses do not have much variation.

    - Players may get bored after playing for a period. I listen something else after an amount of gaming hours.

<br><br>
<p><div class="left-margin" id="set-C"><h2>Last words</h2></div></p>

This game is quite worthy to play. My game play hours have reached above 100 hours and I still feel good on this game.
