<p><div class="left-margin" id="set-C"><h2>Last words</h2></div></p>

For study, it is nice to have good equipment and decent working environment. I don’t think the classroom chairs are good enough to sit for long time, but it seems quite concentrating to have few people in the school and just study there.

擁有好的學習設備和環境幫助到學習。我不認為課室內的椅子是適合長時間坐下，不過只有數名人於學校的環境，看似是一個令人集中的場所。
